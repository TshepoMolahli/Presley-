Welcome to this tribute site for Presley Chweneyagae, the award-winning South African actor known for his role in “Tsotsi”.

About

Born: October 19, 1984
Known For: Tsotsi, The River

Presley was a dedicated actor and activist who brought powerful South African stories to life through his work.

Achievements

  • Starred in Oscar-winning film "Tsotsi"
  • Won SAFTA Best Actor awards
  • Wrote and performed in award-winning plays
